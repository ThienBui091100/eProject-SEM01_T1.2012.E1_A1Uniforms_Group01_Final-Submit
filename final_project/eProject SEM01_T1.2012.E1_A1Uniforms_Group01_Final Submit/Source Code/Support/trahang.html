How To Return The Goods To The Seller / Exshop?

How To Return The Goods To The Seller / Exshop?

1. If the product you buy belongs to Exshop Mall / Favorite Shop / Regular Shop, choose one of the following 3 ways:

- Option 1: Pick up at home (free of charge): The J&T carrier will pick up the return delivery at the address and time you choose

- Option 2: Send goods at J&T post office (free of charge): you pack the goods to be paid and bring it to any J&T post office.

- Option 3: Self-arrange: you pack the goods to be returned and deliver them at another post office, then take a photo of the invoice / receipt and fill in the return information on Exshop app

2. If the product belongs to International Goods:

- You pack the goods to be returned and deliver them at any post office, then fill in the return information on the Exshop.

Note:

- Only proceed to send goods to Exshop / Seller after receiving the notice agreeing to you to accept the return request.

- The buyer needs to package the return item, print and paste the return coupon (emailed) on the package.
